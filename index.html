Quinton Price's Project

Description

For my project, I plan to create a guide for mountain biking and hiking near Seattle. Mountain biking is a passion of mine, something I have grown up with. Often, when looking for new spots, it's hard to find a website that lists trails alongside pictures and detailed information about the ride.

To solve this, I would like to create a website that can act as a travel guide for those coming to the area to bike. Western Washington / British Columbia is a "mecca" for mountain biking, and many people travel here with the sole purpose of experiencing local trails. Having a website that can serve as a guide would be useful to this demographic.

My website would include articles (that serve as guides), pictures, a page where people can submit pictures / reviews of trails and more. I would like to implement a map application that shows the location of trails on said map, hopefully using Google Maps.

Applications

  • As stated previously, I would like to have a discussion area, somewhere where users can submit photos and reviews of trails. Especially in months with inclement weather, having recent reviews and pictures of trail conditions is extremely useful. It can make the difference between a fun and miserable day. I could also potentially include a weather widget in every article that gives conditions for trails.
  • I would also like to have a map feature which allows users to view locations of trails on an interactive map. This would give users the ability to browse trails in the region they intend to ride and, if paired with a service such as Google Maps, potentially see nearby attractions.
  • Additionally, it would be nice to have a service that reads a users' location and suggests rides near them. This is not neccesarily the same as the interactive map, as it would be in the form of a pop-up or link that leads them to articles about different rides.
